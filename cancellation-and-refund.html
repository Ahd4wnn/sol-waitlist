Cancellation & Refund Policy

We aim to keep our policies simple, fair, and transparent. This policy outlines how cancellations and refunds work for Sol.

1. Subscription Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Once cancelled, your subscription will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

After the billing period ends, you will not be charged again.

2. No Partial Refunds

We do not offer partial refunds for unused time within an active subscription period. Once a payment is made, it is considered final for that billing cycle.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only issued under the following circumstances:

• Duplicate or accidental payments
• Technical issues preventing access to the service
• Unauthorized transactions (subject to verification)

All refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

4. Refund Process

To request a refund, contact us within 7 days of the transaction with relevant details.

If approved, refunds will be processed via our payment provider (Razorpay) and may take 5–10 business days to reflect in your original payment method.

5. Payment Gateway

All payments are securely processed through Razorpay. We do not store your card or payment details on our servers.

6. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback without contacting us first may result in suspension of your account. We encourage users to reach out so we can resolve issues quickly and fairly.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our service evolves. Any changes will be posted on this page.

8. Contact
