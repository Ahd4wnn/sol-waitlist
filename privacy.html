Our Promise

Privacy is at the core of Sol. We understand that you may share deeply personal thoughts, and we treat that responsibility with extreme care. Your data is never sold, never exploited, and always handled with security and respect.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide and improve Sol. This includes:

• Email address (for waitlist, updates, and account access)
• Conversation data you choose to share
• Basic technical data such as device type, browser, and usage patterns
• General location data (non-precise, for analytics only)

We do not store voice recordings. If speech is used, it is converted to text and processed securely.

2. Conversation Data & AI Training

Your conversations are private by default. We only use conversation data to improve Sol if you explicitly opt in.

When used for improvement: • Personal identifiers are removed
• Data is anonymized
• It is used strictly to improve AI responses and user experience

Your personal mental health journey is never exposed to other users.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

• Provide and operate the platform
• Personalize your experience
• Improve AI performance and accuracy
• Send important updates (you can opt out anytime)
• Maintain security and prevent misuse

4. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data, including:

• End-to-end encryption of sensitive data
• Restricted access controls
• Secure storage systems
• Regular security audits

Even internally, access to sensitive conversation data is extremely limited.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. Ever.

We may share limited data only in these situations:

• With trusted service providers (for hosting, analytics, etc.)
• If required by law or legal processes
• To protect the safety of users or the platform

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You may:

• Request access to your data
• Request correction of inaccurate data
• Request deletion of your data
• Withdraw consent at any time
• Request a copy of your data (data portability)

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal tracking tools to understand how users interact with Sol and improve the experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may rely on trusted third-party services for infrastructure and analytics. These providers are carefully selected and required to maintain strong privacy standards.

10. Children’s Privacy

Sol is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is identified, it will be deleted immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in different countries depending on our infrastructure. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place regardless of location.

12. Policy Updates

We may update this policy over time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Continued use of Sol means you accept the updated policy.
